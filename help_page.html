How to use this website:

Home Page - convert the currencies of your choice

About Page - learn about the reason why the page was created

Help Page - Leave any questions, or just learn the purpose of each page

Settings Page - change items such as preferred currency, decimal precision, and toggle between light and dark mode.

On this website, you can use voice commands to navigate the different pages and speak different commands, listed below:


Make sure your mic is on by pressing the "Turn on Audio" button.

  • "Hello" - Sends alert saying "Hello World".
    • Say: Hello
  • "Navigate to [page_name]" - Navigates to Home, About, Help, or Settings page
    • Say: Navigate to [page_name]
